About this site

This site shows all the articles from today’s issue of the Guardian or, on Sundays, Observer newspapers. It is run by Phil Gyford and uses the Guardian Open Platform. A new issue appears around midnight UK time.

Navigate the articles using keyboard shortcuts:

Scroll up: w or k Previous article: a or h or ← Next article: d or l or → Scroll down: s or j or space

Clicking a “scroll down” key at the end of an article will take you to the next.

Pressing Shift with “next”/“previous” keys will move you to the next/previous section.

Press v to open the original version of the current article in a new window.

Swiping left/right on phone and tables should move between articles.
